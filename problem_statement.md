Grievance Redressed Management System
The objective of the Grievance Redressal Management System is to develop a centralized, web-based platform for submitting, tracking, and resolving user grievances in an organized and efficient manner. The system enables users to register complaints online and allows administrators to review, manage, and respond to grievances systematically. It aims to improve transparency, accountability, response time, and record management in the grievance handling process.
Objectives

To enable users to register and log in to the system securely.(users-student, faculty, Nonteaching staff, concerned officer).

To provide an online grievance submission form for users.
To allow users to upload supporting documents related to their grievances.
To prioritize grievances based on their category, urgency, or severity to ensure timely resolution.

To allow users to track the status of submitted grievances.

To provide secure login access for the administrator.
To enable the administrator to view, assign, update, and resolve grievances.
To notify users when the status of their grievance is updated.

To provide search and filter functionality for grievances based on category, date, or status.

To generate grievance reports for administrative purposes.

To provide secure logout functionality for users and administrators.
